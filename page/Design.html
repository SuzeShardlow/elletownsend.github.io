DESIGN PORTFOLIO

Graphic design is one of my favourite hobbies. After studying art up until GCSE, I knew I wanted to nourish my creative side while pursuing a more scientifically-based career.

I have designed all the graphics for this site. From choosing the colour scheme, the graphics for my projects and all the typography, I love fuelling my creative passions. If I'm stuck on where to go with a project, or feeling a little uninspired, graphic design and art is where I go to get my drive back.

I have also created poster designs, re-branding for Women In STEM @ University of Hull, and even some stickers!

None of my design projects are public at the moment.
