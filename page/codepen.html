CODEPEN PROJECTS

In order to practice my coding skills, and have a little fun outside of coursework and exam revision, I like to make mini projects on Codepen. I've made some weird and wonderful things, like a script that chooses what video game you should play next, a real life PokeDex and a site that displays different emojis depending on the season!

None of these projects are particularly useful, but they are a way for me to learn and practice new concepts and frameworks.

You can see my retro inspired pens, pens I made for CodePen Challenges, and pens I use to create templates for this website!
