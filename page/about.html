ABOUT ME

I am an Undergraduate at university, studying for a BSc in Computer Science.

I regularly attend hackathons, workshops and conferences. In 2019, I was part of the winning team at CatalystHack, in which we developed a game to encourage more young women into STEM.

Outside of my tech, I like graphic design, video games and reading, I am also an aspiring speaker and blogger.

MY STACK

As well as being a Code First: Girls Instructor - teaching beginner level web development to women aged 18+, I have also been recognised as one of Code First: Girls Ones To Watch 2019!

As vice-president of Hull University Computer Science Society (hullCSS), I organise social events, workshops and meetups for people interested in tech at university. Recently, I hosted 'So You Want To Make Websites' - an introduction to web development talk for students wanting to learn to code.

I have also interned as a web developer, which you can read about here.
