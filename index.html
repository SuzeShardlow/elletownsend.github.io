Who is Elle?

I am an undergraduate computer scientist at the University of Hull, with a passion for front-end web development.

My interest in coding first started when I was introduced to 'Hour of Code' in a school assembly in year 7 which inspired me to start working on little projects of my own. I then went on to persue my interest further at university 7 years on.

Now, I spend most of my time working on projects for my coursework, as well as side projects just for fun. I am a massive advocate for increasing diversity in the tech industry, and was lucky enough to be lead instructor for the AW2018 Code First: Girls Hull Beginners course.

If you want to find out more about what I do day-to-day, you can find me on Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Github.
